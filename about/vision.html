Vision & Mission

The main purpose of establishing the college is to provide education to those students of this region who are deprived of higher education. There was no college here for the students to pursue higher education. While some students managed to go to distant areas for studies, many were left without access to higher education. Considering this, it became very necessary to establish a college. With the establishment of this college, students will be able to avail proper educational opportunities. Holistic development in any region is only possible when the population there is educated, because education is an important link in development.
